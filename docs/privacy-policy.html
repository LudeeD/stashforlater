Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

Information We Collect

StashForLater collects only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Instagram post URLs that you send to our bot
  • Your Instagram username (for associating saved links with your account)
  • Basic interaction data (when you sent links, for organization purposes)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Save and organize the Instagram links you send us
  • Provide you access to your saved links
  • Maintain the functionality of the service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible to you through the bot interface. We do not share your information with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your saved links until you request their deletion or delete them through the bot interface.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your saved data
  • Delete your saved data
  • Request complete account deletion

Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please use our data deletion request page.
